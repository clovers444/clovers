obituary

myspace93 was a myspace clone created by jankenpopp for windows93 in the interest of replicating the atmosphere and appearance of the original myspace circa 2003. it launched in the summer of 2019, ceasing activity on june 29th, 2021, only a month before its 2nd anniversary. prior to expiry, the site amassed 85,680 users—this notable amount owing to tiktok virality—and fostered creativity and expression. an account going by the username missmahirarai was the last to be created, in it can be found links to a website facilitating the hire of call girls and escorts.
